Sparkling Wine · France

Saint Hilaire Blanquette de Limoux

For me, this is a fresh and elegant sparkling wine that feels easy to enjoy. I like its crisp style, fine bubbles and soft fruity notes. It is the kind of bottle I would choose for a warm evening, a relaxed aperitif or a small celebration without making it feel too serious.

Sparkling Wine · Italy

Bellavista Franciacorta Alma Gran Cuvée Brut

For me, Bellavista Franciacorta Alma Gran Cuvée Brut feels elegant, refined and a little more luxurious. I like its creamy style, fine bubbles and richer notes of brioche, pastry and ripe fruit. It is the kind of sparkling wine I would choose for a special dinner, a celebration or a calm evening that should feel more elevated.

Italy 750ml 12.5% 94 Points Chardonnay Pinot Noir Franciacorta

Personal note

Not every sparkling wine has to feel loud.

I like sparkling wines that feel clean, fresh and elegant. For me, they work best when they make a moment feel lighter without becoming too formal.
