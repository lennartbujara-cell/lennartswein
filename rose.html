Rosé Wine · Greece

Thymiopoulos Rosé de Xinomavro 2024

For me, this rosé feels lively, fresh and a little Mediterranean. I like that it is not just simple and sweet, but has more character: red fruit, wild strawberry, cherry, raspberry and a slightly herbal, mineral feeling. It is the kind of rosé I would choose for sushi, fish, vegetarian food or a warm evening where the wine should still feel interesting.

Rosé Wine · France

Clos Cibonne Côtes de Provence Cru Classé Tradition Rosé 2024

For me, this rosé feels more serious, elegant and complex. I like that it has a soft orange-pink color and a richer style with citrus, exotic fruit, ginger, white pepper and subtle rose notes. It is not the kind of rosé I would only drink casually. I would choose it for a better dinner, fish-based dishes, lamb curry or an evening where the wine should feel more refined.

France 750ml 13% 94 Points Organic Tibouren Grenache

Personal note

Rosé should feel light, but not boring.

I like rosé wines that still have personality. For me, a good rosé should feel fresh and easy, but also have enough character to work with food and not just disappear after one sip.
